Albert Einstein

  • 1879 Born in Ulm, Germany on March 14th.
  • 1880 Family moves to Munich.
  • 1895 Renounces German citizenship and enters the Polytechnic School in Zurich.
  • 1900 Graduates from the Polytechnic School with a teaching diploma.
  • 1901 Obtains Swiss citizenship.
  • 1902 Begins work as a patent clerk in Bern, Switzerland.
  • 1903 Marries Mileva MariÄ‡.
  • 1905 Earns his Ph.D. from the University of Zurich.
  • 1912-1914 Lecturer in Theoretical Physics at the Federal Technological Institute of Zurich.
  • 1914 Accepts a professorship at the University of Berlin and membership in the Prussian Academy of Sciences. Family remains in Zurich due to marital issues.
  • 1915 Begins publishing work on the general theory of relativity.
  • 1919 Solar eclipse confirms predictions of general theory of relativity, propelling Einstein to international fame.
  • 1921 Awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics for his explanation of the photoelectric effect
  • 1925-1933 Travels and lectures extensively, promoting science and pacifism.
  • 1955 Dies on April 18th in Princeton, New Jersey.

"No doubt creative genius has many forms, but few if any have been more fertile than that of Einstein."

-- Wolfgang Pauli (Nobel Laureate in Physics)
